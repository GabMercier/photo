The first frost of the season always catches me off guard. There's a narrow window — maybe 30 minutes after sunrise — where the ice crystals are still intact but the light is warm enough to make them glow.

This series was shot in the park behind my apartment, crouching in wet grass at 6:45 AM. Worth it.
